Player Rules

Always 11 players on the pitch and six on the bench

You can only hit the ball with the flat side of your stick

Outfield players are not allowed to use their feet or any other part of their body to control the ball

You must be atleast 5 meters away from the person taking when a free hit is taken

Scoring Rules

A goal is worth 1 point

You can not own goal

You must be inside the D to score a goal

Other Rules

If a game ends in a draw a penalty shuffle is awarded

A penalty shuffle lasts 8 seconds

You must be inside the D to score a goal
